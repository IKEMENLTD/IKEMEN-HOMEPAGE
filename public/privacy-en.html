1. Introduction

IKEMEN Inc. (hereinafter "IKEMEN", "we", "us", or "our") respects the privacy of every individual whose personal information we may process. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect personal information in connection with the services we operate, including TaskMate (taskmateai.net), Mail Eigyo Pro, our corporate website, and any integrations we operate with third-party platforms such as LinkedIn.

We process personal information in accordance with the Japanese Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI, Act No. 57 of 2003, as amended in 2022). Where individuals located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom interact with our services, we voluntarily apply safeguards that are substantively equivalent to those required by the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the UK GDPR.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you provide

  • Account registration data (name, email, company name, role)
  • Billing and payment information (processed by Stripe; we do not store full card numbers)
  • Communications you send to us (support inquiries, sales conversations)
  • Content you upload to TaskMate or Mail Eigyo Pro for the purpose of generating automations or managing campaigns

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and browser information (user agent, language, screen size)
  • Log data (IP address, timestamps, pages visited, referring URL)
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 7)

2.3 Information from LinkedIn integrations

When you authenticate with IKEMEN services using Sign In with LinkedIn, or when you grant us permission to operate LinkedIn Pages or Ad Accounts on your behalf, we receive only the data fields you explicitly authorize through the LinkedIn OAuth consent screen. This may include:

  • Your LinkedIn member identifier (sub) and basic profile (name, profile picture, locale)
  • Your verified email address (if scope email is granted)
  • Aggregated campaign analytics and Lead Gen Form submissions from Ad Accounts where you hold an administrative role
  • Engagement metrics from LinkedIn Pages you administer

We do not collect any LinkedIn member data without explicit member consent obtained through LinkedIn's own consent mechanisms. We do not scrape LinkedIn profiles, harvest connections, or access messaging content.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal information. We share information only with:

  • Sub-processors bound by written data processing agreements: Supabase (database hosting, Tokyo region), Render (application hosting), Vercel (application hosting), Netlify (static hosting and CDN), Cloudflare (network and security), Stripe (payment processing), Anthropic (AI inference, via API), AWS (email delivery via SES).
  • LinkedIn Corporation when you use Sign In with LinkedIn or authorize us to operate Ad Accounts and Pages on your behalf, in accordance with LinkedIn's own privacy practices.
  • Legal authorities when required by valid legal process under Japanese law.

5. International Transfers

Primary data storage is located in Japan (Tokyo / ap-northeast-1). Limited operational data may be transferred to sub-processors operating in the United States and the European Union, in which case we rely on standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: retained for the duration of the customer relationship plus one (1) year, then deleted.
  • Raw API response logs (including LinkedIn responses): 7 days.
  • Aggregated analytics: up to 13 months, after which the data is anonymized.
  • Lead records collected through LinkedIn Lead Gen Forms: retained for the active customer lifecycle plus one (1) year, or until the data subject requests deletion, whichever comes first.
  • Financial records: 7 years, as required by Japanese tax law.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for authentication, session management, and aggregated analytics. Strictly necessary cookies are used without consent. Analytics and marketing cookies are used only with your consent, which you may withdraw at any time through your browser settings.

8. Your Rights

Under APPI, you may request disclosure, correction, suspension of use, or deletion of your personal information. Where applicable, GDPR-equivalent rights including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection are honored on a voluntary basis. To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@ikemen.ltd. We respond within fourteen (14) business days.

9. Security

  • Encryption in transit: TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: AES-256, keys managed by AWS KMS with rotation every 90 days
  • Access control: SSO and two-factor authentication for all internal admin access
  • Quarterly access reviews and annual third-party penetration testing
  • Documented incident response procedures, with notification to affected parties within 72 hours of confirmed material breach

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. LinkedIn-Specific Disclosures

This subsection clarifies practices relevant to LinkedIn members whose data we may process:

  • We process LinkedIn member data only with explicit consent obtained through LinkedIn's own consent mechanisms.
  • We do not use LinkedIn member data to build or augment any standalone profile database, nor do we sell such data to third parties.
  • Upon receipt of a deletion request originating from LinkedIn (including the Lead Sync deletion webhook) or directly from a data subject, we delete the corresponding records in our systems within seven (7) calendar days, log the deletion in an immutable audit trail, and send an acknowledgment to the requester.
  • We do not retain LinkedIn member data beyond the retention periods stated in Section 6.
  • Aggregated analytics derived from LinkedIn campaigns cannot be re-personalized and are retained as aggregated data only.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified to affected users by email and posted on this page at least thirty (30) days before they take effect.
